Clopidogrel versus aspirin for secondary prevention of ischemic stroke: comprehensive evidence review.

Specifically address:
1. CAPRIE trial (1996) — design, primary endpoint, stroke subgroup results, absolute risk reduction, NNT for stroke patients specifically, and limitations/criticisms of the trial.
2. Subsequent meta-analyses comparing clopidogrel vs aspirin monotherapy in secondary stroke prevention (post-CAPRIE), including any Cochrane reviews or individual patient data meta-analyses. Report effect sizes, confidence intervals, NNT where available.
3. Current guideline divergence between ESC/ESOC European guidelines and AHA/ASA American guidelines regarding antiplatelet choice after ischemic stroke — what does each recommend as first-line, what class/level of evidence, and what is the rationale for any differences?
4. Cost-effectiveness data and real-world evidence that may explain guideline divergence (e.g., generic clopidogrel availability timeline, CYP2C19 pharmacogenomics considerations).
5. DAPT (dual antiplatelet) trials (CHANCE, POINT, THALES) — how do these affect the monotherapy clopidogrel vs aspirin comparison?
6. Any 2023-2026 updates or ongoing trials that may shift the evidence landscape.
