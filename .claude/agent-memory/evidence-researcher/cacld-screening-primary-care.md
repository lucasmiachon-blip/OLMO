## Prince 2024 — 83% Clarification

The 83% refers to: among patients found to have ACLD by Liver Toolkit screening, 83% were previously undiagnosed (unaware of liver disease). NOT the overall screening yield.
- Screening yield: 23/179 recalled = 12.8% had ACLD
- Population-level: 23/32,270 = 0.07% total screened
- Independently corroborated by NHANES 2025: >85% of cACLD unaware

## Society Convergence (2024-2025)

EASL 2024, AASLD 2023-2025, ADA 2025, APASL 2024 all converge on FIB-4 as first-line screening in metabolic risk populations. ADA 2025 is the first diabetes society to explicitly recommend routine FIB-4 screening for all T2D/prediabetes.

## Cost-Effectiveness Summary

Most favorable: Park 2024 ICER $14,949/QALY (at-risk NAFLD). All studies below $100K/QALY threshold. SEAL Germany showed direct savings (EUR 2,175/case from earlier detection).
